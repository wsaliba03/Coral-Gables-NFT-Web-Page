NFTs, or Non-Fungible Tokens, are unique digital assets verified using blockchain technology. Unlike cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, each NFT has distinct characteristics and cannot be exchanged on a one-to-one basis.

The City of Coral Gables Trees NFT project aims to celebrate and preserve the beauty of trees in Coral Gables. Each NFT in our collection represents a unique tree that users have voted for from the city.

You can view our NFT collection on our OpenSea page. Each NFT has detailed information including images, Contract Addresses, Token IDs, Token Standards, and Blockchains.

To reserve an NFT, click the “Reserve NFT” button next to the desired NFT. You will be prompted to enter your wallet address. If the reservation is successful, you will receive a confirmation and further instructions.
